Hello, I'm Darren

I'm a Master's student at Santa Clara University, continuing my journey after earning my undergraduate degree here in the Bay Area. I've chosen to build my career in Product Management because I genuinely enjoy solving complex problems and turning ideas into real, impactful products.

I thrive on the challenge of guiding a product through its full lifecycle—from concept and design to launch and iteration. Whether it's building AI-powered features at TikTok or creating my own travel app from scratch, I love the intersection of technology, design, and user experience.

Outside of work, you'll find me playing basketball or soccer, or exploring new places around the world. Travel has always been a huge part of my life—it's what inspired me to build Herd in the first place.
